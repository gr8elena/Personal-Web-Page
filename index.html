I am Elena Shulevska

Student at Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering

As a girl in my 20s I have many desires, since I was little I loved drawing, painting as well as mathematics, thus one of the most difficult decisions in my life appeared. Finki was one of the options where I could express my creativity, but also be in touch with math. I am currently a student at FINKI and a future software engineer. I enjoy doing tasks that FINKI gives me. You can see all the pictures and drawings I have made, as well as the way I express myself in art. If you like any of the displayed you can contact me.
